About Me

I began my career in the United States Navy as a Cryptologic Technician Technical (CTT), where I developed a strong foundation in signals analysis, security, and mission-critical operations. After transitioning from the Navy, I worked as an IT technician, gaining hands-on experience in system administration, troubleshooting, and automation.

I currently serve as a Database Analyst, SAP Security Officer, and Military Analyst. In these roles, I work with database systems, manage access controls and security policies, and support operational analysis. My combined experience in military operations and information technology allows me to approach complex technical problems with both analytical precision and a security-focused mindset.

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University, where I continue to build my skills in software development, data structures, and database design. I am particularly interested in secure system design, database management, and applying technology to real-world operational challenges.
